# Outbound Configuration

Configure your outbound communication strategies to effectively reach your audience. This section guides you through setting up channels and campaigns for proactive engagement using the Growth Marketer agent.

## Configuration Steps

### 1. Channel Setup

Navigate to **Settings > Channels** to configure your communication platforms. The system supports multiple providers:

*   **Email**:
    *   **AgentMail (Recommended)**: Set `agent_email` in your settings. This provides built-in inbox management and tracking.
    *   **SMTP**: Alternatively, configure your own SMTP server details (`host`, `port`, `user`, `pass`) under `email.email`.
*   **WhatsApp**:
    *   **Twilio**: Configure your Twilio account details (`account_sid`, `auth_token`, `phone_number`). Ensure your templates are approved by WhatsApp for outbound initiation.

### 2. Sender Identity

Define your sender profiles to ensure messages are recognizable and trusted:
*   **Email**: Verify your sending domain (especially for AgentMail) to improve deliverability and avoid spam filters.
*   **WhatsApp**: Your business profile name and logo will be visible to recipients.

### 3. Campaign Creation

Campaigns are managed by the **Growth Marketer** agent.

1.  **Define Strategy**: The agent uses your business goals to suggest campaign types (e.g., Lead Generation, User Activation).
2.  **Target Audience**: Campaigns use segments to target specific user groups. Ensure your data sources are connected to populate these segments.
3.  **Content Generation**: The **Copywriter** agent assists in drafting high-converting messages for your chosen channels.
4.  **Launch**: Once approved, the campaign is executed. The system handles message dispatching, respecting channel-specific limits (e.g., WhatsApp's 24-hour window).

## Key Locations

-   **Settings > Channels**: Central configuration for Email, WhatsApp, and other providers.
-   **Agents > Growth Marketer**: The interface for planning, launching, and monitoring campaigns.
-   **Agents > Copywriter**: Tools for generating and refining message content.

## Best Practices

-   **Domain Warm-up**: If using a new email domain, gradually increase sending volume to build reputation.
-   **WhatsApp Templates**: Use pre-approved templates for initiating conversations. Free-form messages are only allowed within the 24-hour response window.
-   **Personalization**: Leverage dynamic fields (e.g., `{{first_name}}`, `{{company}}`) to increase engagement.
-   **Compliance**: Ensure you have user consent before sending outbound messages (GDPR, CAN-SPAM, etc.).
