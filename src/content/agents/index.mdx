# Agents

Makinari provides a suite of specialized AI agents designed to handle specific business functions. Each agent is equipped with tools and capabilities tailored to its role.

## Agent Setup and Context

Agents require specific configurations and context to operate effectively. This includes setting up necessary integrations (like email or messaging platforms) and providing the agent with relevant business information, goals, and constraints. Proper setup ensures the agent can access the right tools and data to perform its tasks accurately.

## Available Agents

- **[CMO](/agents/cmo)**: Chief Marketing Officer for strategy and analysis.
- **[Sales](/agents/sales)**: Lead generation and sales management.
- **[Customer Support](/agents/customer-support)**: Automated customer service.
- **[Data Analyst](/agents/data-analyst)**: Data processing and insights.
- **[Copywriter](/agents/copywriter)**: Content creation and editing.
- **[Email](/agents/email)**: Email management and automation.
- **[WhatsApp](/agents/whatsapp)**: WhatsApp communication handling.
- **[UX](/agents/ux)**: User experience analysis.
- **[Growth](/agents/growth)**: Growth hacking and campaign management.
- **[Chat](/agents/chat)**: Real-time chat interactions.
