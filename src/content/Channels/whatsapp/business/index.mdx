# WhatsApp Business

WhatsApp Business is the professional version of WhatsApp, designed for companies to interact with customers at scale.

## Overview

Connecting a WhatsApp Business account allows you to:
-   Send automated notifications.
-   Provide 24/7 customer support via AI agents.
-   Use verified business profiles for trust.

## Requirements

To use WhatsApp Business with this platform, you must use the **WhatsApp Business API**, not the standard mobile app. This is typically provided through a Business Solution Provider (BSP) like **Twilio**.

### Verification

Meta requires business verification for full access:
1.  Verify your business in **Meta Business Manager**.
2.  Adhere to [WhatsApp Commerce Policy](https://www.whatsapp.com/legal/commerce-policy/).
3.  Maintain a high quality rating to avoid messaging limits.
