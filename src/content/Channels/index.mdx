# Channels

Channels are the communication bridges between your agents and your audience. Makinari supports multiple channels to ensure you can reach your customers where they are.

## Available Channels

- **[Website](/channels/website)**: Integrate a live chat widget on your site.
- **[WhatsApp](/channels/whatsapp)**: Connect via the world's most popular messaging app.
- **[Mail](/channels/mail)**: Manage email communications seamlessly.
- **[Social Networks](/channels/social)**: Unify your social media interactions.
- **[CMS](/channels/cms)**: Manage your content management systems.
